Frames lets you effortlessly record every detail of your shoot, from camera settings and lens information to film stocks, locations, and other important details. Attach references, organize shots, and keep your notes easily accessible—all from your phone.

Frames: The Ultimate Companion for Film Photographers

Capture & Organize

  • Log Essential Metadata: Easily record frame number, focal length, shutter speed, exposure compensation, aperture, flash settings, date, time, and geolocation.
  • Visual Context: Attach a reference image to each frame for quick visual identification.
  • Interactive Map: View your film shots on a map to see where your photography journey has taken you.
  • Organized Library: Use folders and search tools to keep your film library neat and accessible.

Export & Sync

  • Versatile Export Options: Export your metadata as EXIF, CSV, or text files.
  • Seamless Sharing: Share your records effortlessly across Apple devices and contacts.
  • macOS Integration: Sync with the Frames macOS app to reintegrate metadata into your scanned images.

Optimized for Film Photography

  • Adaptive Controls: Controls that match your lens characteristics, supporting full, half, or third aperture clicks.
  • Smart Autofill: Save time with smart autofill for lens, camera, film stock, and ISO.
  • Extensive Film Stock Library: Includes a pre-loaded selection of over 200 film stocks.
  • Intuitive Design: Follows Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines for a smooth and intuitive experience.
  • Performance Optimized: Works seamlessly, even on older iPhone models.

Key Features

  • Film and analog photography metadata management
  • Photo logging and organization
  • Notebook and note-taking features for photography
  • EXIF data integration
  • Camera settings and roll management
  • Frame-by-frame logging
  • Darkroom and shoot planning tools
